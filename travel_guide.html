How to Reach Solapur [cite: 50]

By Road [cite: 51]

Solapur is well connected by highways to major cities such as Pune, Mumbai, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru[cite: 52]. Regular government and private buses operate between these cities[cite: 53]. Distance from major cities: [cite: 54]

  • Pune - 250 km [cite: 55]
  • Hyderabad - 300 km [cite: 56]
  • Mumbai - 400 km [cite: 57]
  • Bengaluru - 380 km [cite: 58]

By Rail [cite: 59]

Solapur Railway Station is a major railway junction. Many trains connect Solapur to cities like Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Bengaluru[cite: 60]. Rail travel is one of the most convenient ways to reach the city[cite: 61].

By Air [cite: 62]

Solapur has a small airport but limited flight services. The nearest major airports are: [cite: 63]

  • Pune International Airport [cite: 64]
  • Hyderabad International Airport [cite: 65]

Travelers can reach Solapur from these airports by road or train[cite: 66].

Local Transport [cite: 67]

Visitors can move around the city using: [cite: 68]

  • Auto rickshaws [cite: 69]
  • Taxis [cite: 70]
  • City buses [cite: 71]

Nearby pilgrimage towns like Pandharpur and Akkalkot are easily accessible by road[cite: 72].
