Famous Dishes [cite: 75]

Jowar Bhakri [cite: 76]

Jowar Bhakri is a traditional flatbread made from sorghum flour. It is commonly served with spicy curries, chutneys, and vegetables. [cite: 77]

Shenga Chutney [cite: 78]

This is a dry peanut chutney made with roasted peanuts, garlic, and spices. [cite: 79] It is often eaten with bhakri or chapati. [cite: 80]

Misal Pav [cite: 81]

Misal Pav is a popular Maharashtrian street food made with spicy sprouted lentil curry served with bread. [cite: 82]

Puran Poli [cite: 83]

Puran Poli is a sweet flatbread filled with jaggery and lentils. It is usually prepared during festivals and special occasions. [cite: 84]
